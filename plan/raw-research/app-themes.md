# Themes & Design

I spend some time browsing on the app store to inspect the themes and designs of slot machine apps. I decided to look at phone apps because it's a common way for people to play slots online.

---

## Common Themes

Overall the themes are recognizable and often mirror the themes of physical slot machines. Seasonal themes are also used (ex: Christmas or Easter)

Here are some of the common themes I have observed:

- Classic
- Ancient Egypt
- Circus
- Ocean and/or Wildnerness
- Mythology (especially ancient Greek and Roman)
- Fruit and/or Food

---

## Common Design Principles

Overall the color scheme is very bright and saturated. There are always clear visual mascots and elements that the user can look at. 

When the user wins there are big splash screen elements and pop ups.
